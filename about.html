About Us

Our Story & Mission

CV By Chiame was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a professional resume, regardless of their background or experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize career success by making professional resume building accessible, fast, and completely free. We believe that a well-crafted resume is the key to unlocking opportunities, and we are dedicated to providing a tool that empowers job seekers at every stage of their career.

We combine modern design principles with user‑friendly technology to ensure that your resume not only looks great but also passes Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) with ease. Whether you are a fresh graduate or an experienced professional, CV By Chiame is here to help you present your best self.

What We Believe

  • Simplicity: Building a resume should be straightforward, not stressful.
  • Accessibility: Everyone deserves a chance, and cost should never be a barrier.
  • Privacy: Your data is yours alone – we never store or share it.
  • Quality: A beautiful, professional design can make all the difference.
  • Empowerment: A great resume opens doors; we want to help you walk through them.

Our Journey

2024 – The Idea

Chiame, a developer and career enthusiast, noticed that many job seekers struggled with expensive and complicated resume tools. The idea for a free, simple, and modern builder was born.

2025 – Development & Beta

After months of design and development, the first version of CV By Chiame was launched to a small group of testers. Feedback was overwhelmingly positive, and the team grew.

2026 – Public Launch

Today, CV By Chiame is open to everyone. We continue to improve, adding more features and templates based on user feedback. Our journey has just begun.
