Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use CV By Chiame.

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CV By Chiame does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All information you enter into the resume form (including personal details, education, experience, skills, and photo) is stored locally in your browser's memory and is not persisted or sent anywhere unless you explicitly choose to download or print your resume.

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not have access to your data. Your resume is yours alone.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it for any purpose. The only processing that occurs is on your own device, where the data is transformed into a formatted resume preview and PDF download. This processing is performed entirely client‑side using JavaScript.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data never leaves your browser. We do not have databases, servers, or any storage mechanisms that capture your information. The security of your data is inherent in this design – we simply do not have it. However, you should be aware that local storage in browsers is not encrypted and can be accessed by other applications or users on the same device. We recommend using our service on a private device.

4. Third‑Party Services

Our website may include links to third‑party sites (e.g., social media buttons). These sites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to read them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external sites.

5. Children's Privacy

CV By Chiame is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action (though we do not store any data, so we will not have any to delete).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We'll be happy to clarify any concerns.

At CV By Chiame, we believe that your data is your own. We designed our system to respect that principle absolutely.
